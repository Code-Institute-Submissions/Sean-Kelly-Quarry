About:

Sean Kelly Quarries Ltd is a family run business, established in 1982 to supply aggregate materials to the local road, farm and building sectors. Based two miles outside of Cootehill, the quarry is ideally situated to supply Monaghan, Cavan, Louth and Meath. As part of the Cootehill formation the quarried stone is predominantly a strong dense Greywacke. From this a full range of high quality aggregates is manufactured to the strictest of standards giving customers the assurance of a quality product
