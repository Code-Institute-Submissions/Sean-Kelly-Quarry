About us:

Histroy

Founded in 1982 by John Kelly, who was the only employee at the time, it was a humble begining. We only offered one product, Shale Rock, and our main customers were local farmers. We have since added a further 13 new products to choose from and have expanded into the consturction and road building sectors.

Who we are?

We are a small family run business, located in a rural area two miles outside of Cootehill. We take great pride in the fact that we are a local business and try to get to know each customer individually so we can tailor our product to their needs.

What we do?

We take only the finest blue Shale, crush and screen it into the various different product sizes, then we perform various tests (includin liguid limit and pyrite tests) to ensure that we only deliever the highest quality products. the tests mentioned above are available on request.
